手部奇物（Hands Slot Wondrous Items）

 

手部奇物由铁手套gauntlets，手套gloves以及其他穿戴在手上的物件组成。

部位：双手

施法者等级：9

激活：无

重量：1磅

效果：当一个魂刃者使用念刃增强(Mind Blade Enhancement)能力的时候，他可以让他的念刃成为一把精金武器，此能力占用+1的增强加值。

先决条件：制造奇物、鬼斧神工（Fabricate）、念刃（MIND BLADE）

制造花费：1000GP、80XP、2天

价格：28,500金币

重量：1磅

效果：这些皮手套上镶嵌着龙牙，像带刺的手套那样造成伤害。它们给予穿戴者的力量+4增强加值。每日三次，角色可以如同她拥有精通击破武器专长那样攻击一件武器或盾牌(即使她不满足先决条件)。

激活：自由 （指令）

重量：2 磅

Each of these white steel gauntlets has a smooth cerulean gem mounted on the wrist.

Soulsmite gauntlets allow a soulborn (MoI 25) to channel the power of a soulmeld into her smite opposition ability. When you use smite opposition, you can select one soulmeld you have shaped. All essentia invested in that soulmeld becomes uninvested, and you gain an additional bonus on your attack roll and damage roll equal to the essentia that was invested. For example, if you were an 8th-level soulborn with 2 essentia invested in your&nbsp; impulse boots, you could drain that soulmeld temporarily to gain an additional +2 bonus on the attack roll and damage roll of your smite attack.

&nbsp; Essentia drained from a soulmeld in this way returns to your essentia pool and can be invested again as normal.

Chakra Bind (Hands): If you bind soulsmite gauntlets to your hands chakra, you gain one additional daily use of your smite opposition ability. When the gauntlets are bound, the gems mounted on them glow with soft inner light. See Magic of Incarnum for rules on binding magic items to your chakras.

先决条件：制造奇物、essentia pool.

制造花费：1,500 gp, 120 XP, 3 days.

Ribbons of electricity crackle over these gleaming, blue-tinged metal gauntlets. Jagged white bolts arc between the pair, filling the air with the scent of ozone.

当把它们一起戴上时，这双+1带刺铁手套使你获得5点闪电抗力。这是一个不需要激活的持续影响。

另外，风暴拳套有4发充能，并且在每天早晨补满。花费1发或更多能让你使用一些以电击为基础的效果。

1发：电爪术

2发：闪电束（反射DC14减半）

4发：闪电法球。这个效果如同炽焰法球，除了你创造的是4个闪耀的闪电法球。你能用同一个动作来指挥任意一个或所有法球。数个法球攻击同个生物会分别造成伤害（反射DC13减半）。这些法球造成电击伤害。

先决条件：制造魔法武器及防具、电爪术、闪电束。

制造花费： 5,000 gp&nbsp; (加上604 gp的一双精制品铁手套费用), 400 XP, 10 days.
